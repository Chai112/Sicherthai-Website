Safety-training, consulting and assesment
to the highest standards.

Sicher provides Process Safety Management (PSM) training, consulting and assessment by our international and national instructors and assessors who has a minimum of 25 years of PSM knowledge and experience in BASF’s world largest chemical company and other large oil and gas organizations. Our company has been certified by Industrial Authority of Thailand (IEAT) and chosen to be one of top tree companies to be in Process Safety Academy Project of IEAT that provide advice for all private companies in Thailand. With our global partner with Pro-Safety Company in Germany and other partners, we are able to provide high service standard of HAZOP study, Hazardous Area Classification and Electrical Selections, Electrical Hazard Assessment (EHA) , Aging Plant Upgrade and many more.
